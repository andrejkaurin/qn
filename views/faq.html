Overview

Are there any upfront recruiting or contractual costs?

For each engagement, we require a $500 deposit that ultimately gets applied to the first invoice. In the event of a failed trial period, the deposit is refunded.

Is it really risk-free?

Yes. All of our weekly engagements begin with a predetermined trial period (up to two weeks). This means that experts have time to prove to you that they are absolutely exceptional and worth every penny. If you’re completely satisfied with the results, we’ll bill you for the time and continue the engagement for as long as you’d like. If you’re not completely satisfied, you won’t be billed (and we'll pay the expert out of our own pocket). From there, we can either part ways, or we can provide you with another expert who may be a better fit and with whom we will begin a second risk-free trial.

What happens if I’m not satisfied with a expert engineer, designer or copywriter?

We allow our clients to try up to five experts per position. While all experts are extraordinarily talented and intelligent, we understand that not every person will be a perfect “cultural” fit for every company. A “cultural” match can be ambiguous as well as highly subjective. For this reason, we allow our clients to work with multiple experts for each position before they decide on a candidate with whom they are confident, content, and comfortable.

What does Qusion.NET offer that comparable services do not?

Qusion.NET was created to offer a solution to the lower-than-expected technical performance of resources obtained through the myriad of talent providers. It’s become incredibly difficult to find reliable engineers and designers through these services due to the lack of sufficient screening.

At Qusion.NET, we thoroughly screen our experts (please see “Testing & Screening” below) to ensure we only supply developers and designers of the absolute highest caliber. To make an analogy, existing open freelance marketplaces are an eBay for developers, whereas Qusion.NET is an Amazon. At Qusion.NET, we go to extreme lengths to provide and guarantee quality.

Testing & Screening

Do all your experts speak fluent English?

Yes - each and every one of them. All experts write and speak English fluently. In fact, before we invite candidates to tackle our rigorous technical tests, we conduct lengthy interviews to ensure they are proficient in English and have personalities well-suited to working with western technical teams.

How exactly do you screen candidates to be sure your software developers meet all of our requirements?

In a nutshell, we screen for language mastery, personality, and technical know-how. We review sample code, algorithmic skills, and CS fundamentals, and we match candidates with senior engineers for multiple technical interviews and a project to demonstrate live proficiency with a “real” project.

How exactly do you screen candidates to be sure your designers meet all of our requirements?

In a nutshell, we screen for language mastery, personality, and design know-how. We review portfolios, check design fundamentals, and test design skills in real-time, and we match candidates with senior designers for multiple design interviews and a project to demonstrate live proficiency with a “real” project.

Geography

Where are your experts located?

While we don’t have any geographic preference, most Experts are physically located in East Europe.

Where do Qusion.NET experts work?

The vast majority of our experts work from their homes or co-working spaces.

Can I bring expert in-house?

In practice, there have only been a few instances where clients have felt it absolutely necessary to bring an expert physically on-site. If this truly is a requirement, we can work with you to coordinate relocating an individual or team to your location for a specified period of time.

Network & Services

If there isn’t any in-network resource currently available to fulfill the requirements of my request, how long should I expect to wait to receive a expert?

It takes us one to three weeks to locate, fully screen, and work with every engineer and designer before we feel confident enough to place them in our network and with a client. We understand your need for expediency, but we also understand the even greater necessity of quality. At Qusion.NET, even if we have an extremely promising prospective candidate, we will not place her/him until we are 100% confident in her/his abilities. Our stringent screening process is the most important reason we’re able to guarantee that our engineers and designers are of the absolute highest caliber.

What happens if an expert is unavailable for the days or times he/she is scheduled to work with me?

We go to every possible length to ensure that this NEVER happens. At Qusion.NET, we completely understand dependability is of the utmost importance. Thus, we take measures to ensure that availability and reliability is a top priority for our experts. However, in the extremely unlikely circumstance that such a situation arises, Qusion.NET will credit back the time billed to the client.

Can I hire a full-time worker from us and bring him/her into our company?

The short answer is “yes.” That said, we’ll want to work with you to fully understand your specific requirements so that we can establish a mutually beneficial arrangement.

Do you offer services such as screening, vetting, and testing to help us hire engineers?

While we currently do not supply these services, we plan on offering them in the future.

Is there any standard equipment experts are required to have?

In order for our experts to stay on the same page as the peer employees they’ll be placed with, we require they work with modern equipment. If we feel a expert’s equipment isn’t quite up to par, we’ll help him/her acquire a new laptop and peripherals.

Intellectual Property

Who owns the legal rights to technology or design created by a your expert?

The client. Qusion.NET in the business of supplying experts of the highest caliber, not collecting intellectual property. All work created by a Qusion.NET is the property of the client.

How do we ensure that our IP is protected?

Our experts are no different from anyone on your team in terms of legal status and their ownership of the product you create. Any non-compete or non-disclosure agreements are made between you and the expert. We do our best to provide forms and legal documents in order to facilitate and streamline these processes. You’re welcome to use your own when entering into an agreement with a our expert; however, our legal division will need to review the paperwork first.
