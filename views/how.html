1

TELL US WHAT KIND OF TECH TALENT YOU NEED

Do you need developers, designers or copywriter? Which type of experts do you need?

Submit a job to let us know what you need — the more details the better. Whether it's a single engineer or designer or a cross-functional team of ten, Qusion.NET can handle it. After we get the job description, our internal team of engineers and designers will review it and connect with you in order to answer any questions and to get an even better understanding of your exact needs.

2

WE'LL FIND YOU THE PERFECT MATCH

We'll notify you with a status report concerning your request within a few days.

We have someone in our network who is ready to work with you immediately.
There is a candidate being evaluated who matches your qualifications, and we will notify you upon the completion of the screening process.
We currently do not have a match for your request, so our team of engineers will work tirelessly to find one.

3

THEY BECOME PART OF YOUR TEAM

We’ll introduce you to your experts, and they’ll ramp up and start working as soon as you say go.

As soon as you review and sign-off on our recommended freelancer(s), they'll be ready to integrate into your team — just like an in-house employee. While our experts usually work remotely, arrangements can be made to have them on-site.

4

WORK WITH THEM 100% RISK-FREE

If you're not 100% satisfied after a trial working with a expert, we'll start the process all over again at absolutely no cost.

Our risk-free trial period lets companies work with our engineers and designers first before deciding if they're 100% confident in moving forward. If, for whatever reason, you are not completely satisfied with a expert you have been paired with, you will not be liable for any payment, and we can either part ways or we will restart the entire cycle with you at absolutely no cost.
